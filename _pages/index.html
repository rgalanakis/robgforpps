We deserve good schools, safe streets, and healthy communities.

My name is Rob Galanakis. I'm a proud Portland resident and PPS parent of two. I know Portland can again be a world-class city, and to do that we need world-class schools.

But our public schools are in crisis, and we need more than the same approach that got us into this mess.

We need to be bold. We need to be creative.

Pointing fingers and begging for bailouts isn't going to rebuild a great public school system.

We know our schools are under-funded, but we also need to answer: What can we do to help our kids thrive today?

Policy Matters

There's no silver bullet to this education crisis. It will take more ideas and creativity than we have offered so far.

PPS needs to go beyond treating School Policy as only Education Policy.
