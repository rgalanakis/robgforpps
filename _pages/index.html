Portlanders deserve good schools, safe streets, and healthy communities.

My name is Rob Galanakis. I’m a proud Portland resident and PPS parent of two.

My platform is called One City. I believe that, to get out of the multipronged crises we are in, we must act with urgency and creativity across our school, city, and county governments.

Will this be a challenge? Yes. Will things go wrong? Yes. Is this idealistic? Yes.

But it’s also the only realistic option we have. We don’t have the money, we don’t have the time, we don’t have the resources.

The only impossible approach is to keep the status quo.

I’m running for Portland School Board to bring bold, creative leadership to a district that has for too long looked too internally.

To add an engaged, willing partner ready to collaborate with the City and County on solving our hardest problems, including climate, housing, and transportation.

To bring a sense of extreme urgency to the disasters we’re leaving our kids in terms of their economic and environmental futures.

The challenges ahead are hard. The stakes are high.
But we don’t have a choice.
We only have one planet.
One school district.
One Portland.

One City.

One City

Our problems have been decades in the making, and they won’t be fixed overnight.

PPS is part of One City. That means we need to look at problem solving from the perspective of the city as a whole, and expect our partners do the same.
