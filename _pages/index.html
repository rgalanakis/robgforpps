Elect Rob G for great schools, safe streets, and healthy communities.

My name is Rob Galanakis. I’m running for Portland Public Schools Board to make sure our kids get the best education we can provide so that they can be successful in life.

Their success is our success.

Making sure our City is safe, healthy, and full of opportunity is critical to that success as well. I will work with our partners — the City, County, and State along with business and community leaders — to create a place where our kids can thrive.

On May 20th, vote for Rob “G” Galanakis, for your kids, schools, and city.
