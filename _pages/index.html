”We’ve tried nothing, and we’re all out of ideas“ should be a joke, not policy.

Our public schools are in crisis, and we can’t problem solve with the same approach that got us into this mess.

I am a proud Portland resident and PPS parent of two. I know Portland can again be a world-class city, but to do that we need world-class schools.

We need to be bold and creative in our solutions.

Pointing fingers and begging for bailouts isn't going to rebuild a thriving public school system.

Public schools are at the center of our communities. We'll create better schools and healthier communities when we engage with the incredible energy of Portland.

Policy Matters

There's no silver bullet to this education crisis. It will more ideas and creativity than we have offered so far. PPS needs to go beyond treating School Policy as only Education Policy.
